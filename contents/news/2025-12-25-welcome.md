Welcome to XDYou, the comprehensive campus application designed specifically for Xidian University students!

## About XDYou

XDYou (西电You) is your essential companion for navigating campus life at Xidian University. We understand the challenges students face in managing their academic and daily activities, and we're here to make your life easier.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless, integrated platform that brings together all the essential services you need as a Xidian student. From checking your course schedule to finding your way around campus, XDYou has got you covered.

## What Makes XDYou Special?

- **All-in-One Platform**: Access all campus services in one place
- **Real-Time Updates**: Get instant notifications about schedule changes and important announcements
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Clean, intuitive design that's easy to navigate
- **Offline Support**: Access key features even without internet connection
- **Regular Updates**: We're constantly improving based on your feedback

## Coming Soon
